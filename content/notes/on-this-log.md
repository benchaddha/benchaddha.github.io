This research log will document my ongoing work and current interests. 

Its purpose is to publish process. In an environment increasingly shaped by generated outputs, the structure of thinking is often obscured. This log attempts to make that structure visible.

Entries are provisional by design. Most notes will record partial results, sketches, ideas, and working hypotheses rather than finished conclusions. When something is complete, it will usually appear elsewhere on the site.

The primary focus is evaluation and infrastructure for AI agents. Occasional notes on computational music systems and other thoughts may appear. I may also eventually publish some material from earlier work.
